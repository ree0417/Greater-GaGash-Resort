At GGR, our pool area is designed for ultimate relaxation and indulgence. Surrounded by lush tropical landscaping, the crystal-clear infinity pool offers panoramic views, blending seamlessly with the horizon. Comfortable loungers and private cabanas provide guests with their own serene space to unwind, whether soaking up the sun or enjoying a refreshing drink. The poolside bar serves a variety of cocktails, mocktails, and light bites, perfect for refreshing breaks between swims. As the sun sets, the ambient lighting transforms the area into a tranquil oasis, making it an ideal spot for relaxation day and night.

The dining area at GGR is a culinary retreat, offering guests an elegant yet inviting atmosphere to enjoy our gourmet offerings. With large windows that frame breathtaking views, the space is designed to bring the beauty of the surrounding landscape indoors. Guests can enjoy a range of seating options, from intimate tables to group-friendly arrangements, all thoughtfully arranged to balance privacy with a lively dining atmosphere.

Our chef-curated menu combines locally sourced ingredients with international flavors, offering dishes that cater to diverse palates. Whether dining under soft, ambient lighting in the evenings or enjoying a bright, sunlit breakfast, every meal is a memorable experience.

At GGR, our guestrooms are designed to provide the perfect blend of luxury and comfort, ensuring a restful stay. Each room features elegant décor, plush bedding, and modern amenities, creating a serene retreat for guests. Floor-to-ceiling windows offer stunning views of either the sparkling ocean or the lush gardens, allowing you to wake up to nature’s beauty every morning. For added comfort, rooms are equipped with spacious seating areas, flat-screen TVs, and high-speed Wi-Fi, perfect for both relaxation and work.

Whether you're enjoying a peaceful night’s sleep or indulging in room service, the guestrooms at GGR Resort provide a quiet and luxurious sanctuary to retreat to after a day of adventure.
